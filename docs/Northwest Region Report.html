This report is generated based on hospitalization data through 01/19/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 01/06/2021.

The Northwest LPHA region includes Jackson, Mesa, Moffat, Rio Blanco and Routt counties.
