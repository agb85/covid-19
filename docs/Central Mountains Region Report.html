This report is generated based on hospitalization data through 01/11/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 12/29/2020.

The Central Mountains LPHA region includes Eagle, Garfield, Grand, Pitkin and Summit counties.
