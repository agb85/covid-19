Effective reproductive number (Re) reflects hospitalization data through 04/26/2021. In regions with smaller populations, reported cases are also used to generate these estimates. The prevalence of infections and percent of the population infected is estimated for 04/19/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 04/13/2021.

The Central Mountains LPHA region includes Eagle, Garfield, Grand, Pitkin and Summit counties.
