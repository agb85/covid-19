This report is generated based on hospitalization data through 12/14/2020. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 12/01/2020.

The Central Mountains LPHA region includes Eagle, Garfield, Grand, Pitkin, and Summit counties.
