Effective reproductive number (Re) reflects hospitalization data through 03/08/2021. In regions with smaller populations, reported cases are also used to generate these estimates. The prevalence of infections and percent of the population infected is estimated for 03/01/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 02/23/2021.

The Southwest LPHA region includes Archuleta, Dolores, La Plata, Montezuma and San Juan counties.
