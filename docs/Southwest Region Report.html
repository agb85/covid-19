This report is generated based on hospitalization data through 02/08/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 01/26/2021.

The Southwest LPHA region includes Archuleta, Dolores, La Plata, Montezuma and San Juan counties.
