Effective reproductive number (Re) reflects hospitalization data through 03/01/2021. In regions with smaller populations, reported cases are also used to generate these estimates. The prevalence of infections and percent of the population infected is estimated for 02/22/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 02/16/2021.

The West Central Partnership LPHA region includes Delta, Gunnison, Hinsdale, Mineral, Montrose, Ouray and San Miguel counties.
