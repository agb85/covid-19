This report is generated based on hospitalization data through 02/01/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 01/19/2021.

The Southeast LPHA region includes Baca, Bent, Crowley, Kiowa, Otero and Prowers counties.
