Effective reproductive number (Re) reflects hospitalization data through 03/22/2021. In regions with smaller populations, reported cases are also used to generate these estimates. The prevalence of infections and percent of the population infected is estimated for 03/15/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 03/09/2021.

The San Luis Valley LPHA region includes Alamosa, Conejos, Costilla, Rio Grande and Saguache counties.
