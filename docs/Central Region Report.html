Effective reproductive number (Re) reflects hospitalization data through 06/07/2021. In regions with smaller populations, reported cases are also used to generate these estimates. The prevalence of infections and percent of the population infected is estimated for 05/31/2021. Due to lags between infection and hospitalization, estimates of transmission control and the effective reproductive number represent the spread of infections through approximately 05/25/2021.

The Central LPHA region includes Chaffee, El Paso, Lake, Park and Teller counties.
